Bard

A priest of the Old Faith, wielding the powers of nature and adopting animal forms. Druids command primal magic to heal allies, control the battlefield, and unleash the fury of the natural world.

A master of martial combat, skilled with a variety of weapons and armor. Fighters are the most versatile and reliable combatants, excelling in sustained damage and battlefield control.

A holy warrior bound to a sacred oath, combining martial prowess with divine magic. Paladins are the most burst-damage-oriented class, using Divine Smite to unleash devastating radiant strikes.

Ranger

A warrior who uses martial prowess and nature magic to hunt threats at the edge of civilization. Rangers excel at ranged combat, exploration, and tracking favored enemies across diverse terrain.

Rogue

A scoundrel who uses stealth and trickery to overcome obstacles. Rogues are masters of Sneak Attack, dealing massive single-target damage when striking from advantage or with an ally nearby.

Sorcerer

A spellcaster who draws on inherent magic from a bloodline or other origin. Sorcerers are raw magical powerhouses who use Metamagic to twist and enhance their spells in unique ways.
d8Charisma

Warlock

A magic-user who draws power from a pact with an otherworldly patron. Warlocks have limited but always-available spell slots that recharge on short rest, plus powerful cantrips and invocations.

Wizard

A scholarly magic-user capable of manipulating the structures of reality. Wizards have the largest spell list in the game and learn spells from scrolls, making them the most versatile casters.
