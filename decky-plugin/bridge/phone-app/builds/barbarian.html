Barbarian

A fierce warrior of primitive background who can enter a battle fury. Barbarians are primal combatants who rely on raw physical power and their iconic Rage to devastate foes on the battlefield.

Class Features

Level 1
Lv 1Rage

Enter a battle fury that grants advantage on Strength checks, resistance to bludgeoning/piercing/slashing damage, and bonus damage on melee attacks. Lasts 10 rounds.

Lv 1Unarmored Defense

While not wearing armor, your AC equals 10 + Dexterity modifier + Constitution modifier. You can use a shield and still gain this benefit.

Level 2
Lv 2Reckless Attack

On your first attack on your turn, you can attack recklessly. You gain advantage on melee attack rolls using Strength during this turn, but attack rolls against you have advantage until your next turn.

Lv 2Danger Sense

You have advantage on Dexterity saving throws against effects you can see, such as traps and spells. You lose this benefit if you are blinded, deafened, or incapacitated.

Level 5
Lv 5Extra Attack

You can attack twice instead of once when you take the Attack action on your turn.

Lv 5Fast Movement

Your speed increases by 10 feet while you are not wearing heavy armor.

Level 7
Lv 7Feral Instinct

You have advantage on Initiative rolls. If you are surprised at the beginning of combat, you can act normally on your first turn if you enter Rage.

Level 9
Lv 9Brutal Critical

When you score a critical hit with a melee weapon, you can roll one additional weapon damage die.
